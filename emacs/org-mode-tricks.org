* ADD LINK IN ORG-MODE
** Link Format
#+begin_src txt
[[link][description]]
[[http://www.baidu.com][baidu]]
#+end_src

** Anchor Point
#+begin_src txt
<<A>>
#+end_src

** Internal Links
The anchor point can be used as a link.
#+begin_src txt
[[A][toA]]
#+end_src

** External links
#+begin_src txt
[[http://www.baidu.com][http]]
[[file:~/.emacs.d/init.el][file]]
#+end_src

** Target Link

** Keybindings
| keybindings | description        |
|-------------+--------------------|
| =C-c C-l=   | Create/Edit a link |
|-------------+--------------------|
| =C-c C-o=   | Open a link        |
